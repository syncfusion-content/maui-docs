# Features: SfTabView

Use these pages to implement common `SfTabView` scenarios.

## Categories

- **Data Binding** (`data-binding.md`)
  - Bind `ItemsSource`, build headers/content with templates, and bind `SelectedIndex`.

- **Customization & Theming** (`customization-theming.md`)
  - Customize tab bar background, indicator, borders, sizing, and `VisualStateManager` states.

- **Selection & Interaction** (`selection-interaction.md`)
  - Handle `SelectionChanging`, `SelectionChanged`, `TabItemTapped`, and enable swiping/ripple.

- **Layout & Sizing** (`layout-sizing.md`)
  - Switch between Top/Bottom/Left/Right tab bars and control sizing modes.

- **Accessibility & RTL** (`accessibility-rtl.md`)
  - Use `FlowDirection` and app accessibility primitives with tabs.

- **Platform Specifics** (`platform-specifics.md`)
  - Apple-only liquid glass effect and other platform notes.
